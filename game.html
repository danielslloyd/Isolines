How to play
  • Walls: click to place points, Enter or double-click to build, right-click to undo, Esc to cancel. The engine snaps your wall onto ridges.
  • High ground is armor: walls on hills take less damage, towers on hills and walls shoot farther.
  • Enclose houses completely to protect them — circled houses are exposed. Protected civilians pay taxes each wave.
  • Add a gate to a walled enclosure for +25% trade income; rams will head for it.
  • Attackers besiege your weakest, lowest stretch of wall. Watch the red circles.
  • Each level the village grows outward — extend the walls, upgrade palisades to stone, stone to fortified.
  • Eras escalate: Bronze Age raids → Iron Age rams → medieval catapults, sappers, siege towers, trebuchets.
  • Space starts the next wave. You can build during a siege if enemies aren't standing on the site.
